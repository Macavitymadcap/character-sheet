# Special Operations

**Skill Proficiencies:** Stealth, Deception

**Tool Proficiencies:** Disguise kit, forgery kit

**Equipment:** An insignia of rank (kept hidden), a set of traveler's clothes, a set of common clothes for blending in, a disguise kit, a forgery kit, and a pouch containing 10 gp

### Background Features

## Special Ops

Your special forces training and knowledge of urban military operations gives you a dual advantage. You can invoke your former rank to influence soldiers and officials (though doing so risks exposure), and your spec ops background means you know how to navigate cities, identify surveillance, find safe houses, and recognize other intelligence operatives. You can usually gain an audience with military personnel or find black market contacts when you need them; though each use is a calculated risk.
